## 📌 Project Overview
In the hyper-growth ecosystem of E-commerce, customer support is the frontline of brand loyalty. This project presents a high-fidelity analysis of **Salla’s** call center operations, processing over **870,000+ data points** to extract meaningful operational patterns.

The primary challenge was not just visualizing the data, but converting massive interaction volumes into a tool that helps managers make "split-second" staffing and coaching decisions. This dashboard serves as the operational heartbeat of the department.

---

## 🎯 Strategic Objectives
* **Demand Forecasting:** Bridging the gap between **Calls Offered (876K+)** and **Calls Handled (857K+)**.
* **Quality Assurance:** Maintaining a world-class **90%+ Service Level (SL)** across all shifts.
* **Cost Optimization:** Reducing the **Abandon Rate (2.15%)** to maximize customer retention.
* **Workforce Management:** Identifying peak hours to prevent both under-staffing and agent burnout.

---

## 🛠 Technical Stack & Tools
* **Data Engine:** Power BI Desktop (Latest Version).
* **ETL Layer:** Advanced Power Query for cleaning, pivoting, and normalizing messy call logs.
* **Calculations:** DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) for dynamic Time-to-Answer and SL% metrics.
* **Modeling:** Optimized Star Schema to ensure high performance with nearly 1 million rows.
* **UX/UI:** Custom theme design for dark-mode high-contrast readability.

---

## 📊 Key Analytical Features
* **North Star Gauge:** A top-level comparison of call volume vs. resolution capacity.
* **Real-time SL% Tracker:** A dynamic visual that alerts when the service level drops below the 90% threshold.
* **Temporal Heatmap:** A "Day vs. Hour" matrix showing when the system is under the most pressure.
* **The Agent Matrix:** A specialized view to track individual productivity without micro-managing.
* **Answer Time Analysis:** Granular breakdown of the first 20 seconds of every interaction.

---

## 📈 Expert-Level Insights
* **Operational Stability:** Despite massive volume, the system maintains a **2.15% Abandon Rate**, indicating high efficiency.
* **Peak Identification:** Data revealed that Saturdays and Mondays have a **15% higher surge** than midweek days.
* **Staffing Gap:** Identified a specific 2-hour window (2 PM - 4 PM) where wait times increased by 10%.
* **Coaching Opportunities:** Isolated a correlation between tenure and "First Call Resolution" (FCR).

---

## 📂 Dataset Architecture
The dataset is structured to provide high granularity across multiple dimensions:
* **Fact Table:** Individual call logs (Offered, Handled, Abandoned, Duration).
* **Agent Dimension:** Name, ID, Team Lead, and Skill Level.
* **Time Dimension:** Breaking data down by Shift, Hour, Day, and Month.
* **Volume:** Managing data integrity for over **876,000 unique records**.
