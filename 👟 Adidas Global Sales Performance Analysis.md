## 📌 Project Overview
Data isn't just rows in Excel; it’s the compass for global management. This project isn't just a presentation of sales numbers—it’s a comprehensive analytical experience designed to answer real-world business questions for **Adidas**.

By analyzing multi-channel sales (In-store, Online, Outlet), we’ve built a dashboard that identifies not just *what* was sold, but *why* certain trends shifted. This reflects the philosophy of **Mazen Analytics Lab**: The story is always in the details, not just the overall picture.

---

## 🎯 Strategic Objectives
* **Channel Optimization:** Evaluate the dominance of In-store sales ($356.7M) vs. digital expansion.
* **Product Strategy:** Investigate why "Footwear" lags behind "Apparel" in physical stores.
* **Seasonal Forecasting:** Analyze MoM growth patterns to predict Q4 peaks and Q1 dips.
* **Customer Journey:** Segmenting performance by gender and athletic category (Men’s Athletic vs. Women’s Apparel).

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Methodology
* **Power BI & DAX:** Engineering complex measures for **Month-over-Month (MoM)** growth and Year-to-Date (YTD) comparisons.
* **UI/UX Design:** Crafting a "Simple yet Deep" interface for seamless executive navigation.
* **Data Cleaning (Power Query):** Meticulous normalization of retail records to ensure 100% data integrity.
* **Business Logic:** Implementing seasonal growth analysis to identify market volatility.

---

## 📊 Key Features & Functionality
* **The Revenue Pulse:** Real-time tracking of In-store leads ($356.7M) across 1,174+ orders.
* **Smart Observation Engine:** Automated insights pointing out discrepancies in Footwear display strategies.
* **Peak Period Analysis:** Comparative view of Dec-April (2022) vs. Dec-March (2021) trends.
* **Category Deep-Dive:** Interactive slices for Men's Apparel and Athletic Footwear performance.
* **Dynamic Navigation:** Side-bar navigation for a "Website-like" user experience.

---

## 📈 Executive Summary of Insights
* **Channel Leader:** In-store remains the "King" of revenue, but requires a strategic review of Footwear placement.
* **Growth Shifts:** 2022 saw a massive shift in April performance compared to 2021, suggesting a change in consumer habits.
* **Top Performers:** Men's Apparel consistently drives the highest volume, acting as a "Magnet Product" for other categories.
* **The "March Mystery":** Data identified a significant decline in March 2022, prompting a deeper investigation into supply chain or promotional impacts.

## 📂 Dataset Description
The analysis leverages the official Adidas Sales Dataset:
* **Dimensions:** Retailer, Region, State, City, Product Category.
* **Metrics:** Price per Unit, Units Sold, Total Sales, Operating Profit, Operating Margin.
* **Timeline:** Comprehensive data covering 2021 and 2022 performance cycles.
